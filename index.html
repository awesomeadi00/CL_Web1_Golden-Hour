Golden Hour

About

  • Hey there! My name's Aditya Pandhare and i'm studying Computer Science and Interactive Media! But if i'm being honest I love making games, XR experiences and AI. Some of my hobbies include making music as I play the guitar, I love to skateboard, I also love taking walks and going on random strolls anywhere really.
  • In fact, that's how I came about the idea for this website. Upon strolling around campus, I realized that I often like to walk around during sunset because I personally feel like Abu Dhabi has some of the most beautiful sunsets. I find myself walking around during sunset to admire the beauty of nature and also just detatch from everything and take a breather.
  • Hence, this video and this website is dedicated to that special moment in time, the golden hour period during sunset or sunrise. The magical moment where time stops for me and I can feel at peace with everything.
